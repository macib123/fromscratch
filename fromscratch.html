Top 5 Disney Movies
(A list compiled entirely based off my own opinion)
    5. The Little Mermaid (1989)
    • Fun Fact: The Little Mermaid was the last feature film to be animated using hand-painted animation. Digital animation came along shortly after this movie was completed. It was the end of an era for Disney.

    4. Coco (2017)
    • Fun Fact: This film took 6 years to produce! A lot of research went into creating the culrute of this movie They wanted to make a fun and relatable film while still accurately representing the mexican culture.

    3. Ratatouille (2007)
    • Fun Fact: The evil character Bomb Voyage from the incredibles makes a cameo in ratatouille. He shows up as a mime in the streets of Paris.

    2. Princess and the Frog (2009)
    • Fun Fact: Mama Odie's character is partially inspired by a well loved comedian and paritally by Yoda! The film took 3 years to complete.

    1. Lion King (1994)
